Games of Moria

Gather the knowledge

Welcome to the Games of Moria! This is a memory card game based on the characters of Middle Earth. Simply click on the 'Adventure Awaits!' button, solve the riddle and press Enter. Find the pairs of each character through flipping the images over. Hint: if you are new to this world, the password is 'mellon' (elvish word for friend).

Additions

Once in game you can opt to use a timer, which will give you 30 seconds to complete the game. Should you fail to do so, just press the Restart button! May Eru bless you with the speed of the Mearas!
